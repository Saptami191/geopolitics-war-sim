## ⚙️ Core Cabinet Subsystems (F1–F6)

Unlock six high-precision operational vectors to pilot your selected nation:

### 🏛️ 1. Government (F1)
*   **Fiscal Regulation**: Fine-tune your nation's general Tax Rate to generate billion-dollar cash revenue stream reserves.
*   **Approval & Stability**: Control social unrest, suppress popular riots, and satisfy rival domestic political factions.
*   **Faction Balance**: Manage individual satisfaction levels across distinct ideological blocks including Military, Oligarchy, Populist, and Tech-Intelli cadres.

### 🏦 2. Central Bank (F2)
*   **Policy Interest Rates**: Shift base discount rates to cool over-inflated economies or inject capital during heavy debt default triggers.
*   **Bond Markets**: Sell treasury debt to secure immediate slush funding or buy back outstanding bonds during cash surpluses.
*   **Reserve Trading**: Speculate on global resources like sweet Crude oil barrels and physical gold reserves to cushion sovereign currency scores.

### 🚀 3. Arsenal (F3)
*   **Defense Procurement**: Build tactical arsenals consisting of high-payload ICBMs, SLBM ballistic submarines, tactical bombers, and intercept defenses.
*   **Active Defense Matrix**: Maintain intercept readiness percentages to automatically neutralize incoming ballistic missile coordinates.
*   **Deployment Controls**: Lock targeting telemetry onto rival nations to prepare defensive borders or pre-emptively deploy deterrent measures.

### 🤝 4. Diplomacy (F4)
*   **Trade Alliances**: Sign deep trade partnerships to boost GDP growth velocity across participating networks.
*   **Defense Pacts**: Interlink collective security systems to shield smaller allies or construct unified geopolitical alliances.
*   **Escalation Controls**: Launch comprehensive market embargoes to isolate rival economies or declare direct war terms.

### 🔬 5. Research (F5)
*   **Technology Tree**: Unlock specialized military-industrial assets (Anti-Ballistic Missiles, Advanced Stealth hulls, Space Laser Recon) and soft domestic upgrades (Deep Quantum Cryptography, Agricultural Soil Optimization).
*   **Hefty Dividends**: Upgraded nodes unlock additional passive stat multipliers and defensive telemetry grids.

### 🕵️ 6. Intelligence & Espionage (F6)
